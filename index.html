Every shooting is a mass shooting

Four-year-old Will hears gunshots outside of his window. He gets scared and cries himself to sleep. Gunshots become a familiar neighborhood sound. By sixteen, he has his own gun. So do all of his friends. One bullet shatters more lives than you think.
